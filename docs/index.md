# NMDC BERmap

An interactive catalog of **DOE BER** (Biological and Environmental Research) research
programs — Bioenergy Research Centers, Scientific Focus Areas, and User Facilities — with their
studies, datasets, publications, and harmonized variables.

## What's inside

- **46 programs** — GLBRC, JBEI, CABBI, CBI (BRCs); ENIGMA, m-CAFEs, µBiospheres, InCoGenTEC and
  other Genomic Science SFAs; Watershed Function, River Corridor and other ESS SFAs; and the
  JGI / EMSL / KBase / ESS-DIVE / NMDC user facilities.
- **81 studies** and **89 datasets** with NMDC IDs, BioProject accessions, and external dataset links.
- **547 variables** with units, roles, ontology mappings (MIxS / BERVO / ENVO / NCBITaxon / …),
  and grounded categorical levels — see the [Variable Browser](variables.html) and the
  [association diagrams](associations.html).
