## env_triad — heterogeneous and conceptually right

Unlike other studies in this batch, this one uses **three distinct env triad combinations** that correctly reflect the three sample compartments mentioned in the title:

| Compartment | n | broad | local | medium |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Bulk soil | 109 | terrestrial biome [ENVO:00000446] | garden [ENVO:00000011] | bulk soil [ENVO:00005802] |
| Rhizosphere | 108 | terrestrial biome | rhizosphere [ENVO:00005801] | soil [ENVO:00001998] |
| Root | 101 | terrestrial biome | environment associated with a plant part or small plant [ENVO:01001057] | root matter [ENVO:01000349] |

(plus 98 samples with other combinations not shown above — likely additional compartments or replicate batches.)

NMDC distinguishes the three compartments here — better curation than `sty-11-vh2hty57` (uniform bulk soil) or `sty-11-wbc14h22` (uniform farm soil).

The bermap profile's `PlantCompartmentEnum` ranges over `root endosphere` / `rhizosphere` / `soil`, with `root endosphere → GOLDPATH:5304`. Consistent with how the data is stored.

## Design factor in `samp_name`

Sample names carry Populus genotype + plot info:

```
BESC-904-Co3_16_51 rhizosphere
GW-9591-CL1_34_16 rhizosphere
GW-9591-Co2_62_24 rhizosphere
```

- `BESC-NNN` / `GW-NNNN` = Populus genotype IDs (BESC = Bioenergy Science Center; GW = …)
- `Co3` / `Co2` / `CL1` = plot codes within the common garden
- Trailing `rhizosphere` / `endosphere` / `bulk soil` = compartment (echoed by `env_local_scale`)

The bermap profile's `PopulusGenotypeEnum` has a placeholder value "27 P. trichocarpa genotypes" (null grounding) — that should be reified into the actual list of 27 BESC and GW genotype codes by parsing all `samp_name` values.

## Open items

- 27 Populus genotypes are stored in `samp_name` but not surfaced as a structured NMDC field. The bermap profile's `PopulusGenotypeEnum` could be populated from a `samp_name` parse pass.
- Plot codes (Co/CL series) likewise unsurfaced.
- bermap profile slot annotations should flag the env_triad heterogeneity as a feature, not a bug — this is one of the cleaner NMDC ingests in the cohort.

## Known limitations of this audit

- The "98 other combinations" sample subset wasn't drilled into; could include additional compartments (e.g. shoot, leaf) or sentinel/control records.
